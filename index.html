WELCOME TO FAZBEAR ENTERTAINMENT.

Who are we?

Since its humble beginnings, Fazbear Entertainment has been dedicated to delivering world-class family entertainment. What started as a single restaurant, Freddy Fazbear’s Pizza, quickly grew into a beloved name in the industry. Featuring animatronic characters like Freddy Fazbear™, Bonnie the Bunny™, Chica the Chicken™, and Foxy the Pirate Fox™, the restaurant became a household favorite. Over time, the brand expanded to include Fazbear Frights, an immersive experience designed to bring guests closer to the magic of our animatronics.

Innovation and Expansion

As technology advanced, Circus Baby’s Entertainment and Rentals introduced state-of-the-art animatronics for both public and private events, offering an experience unlike any other. Fazbear Entertainment continued to evolve, leading to the creation of Freddy Fazbear’s Mega Pizzaplex—a massive entertainment complex filled with arcade games, dining options, and grand performances, solidifying our reputation as leaders in family entertainment.

Today, Fazbear Entertainment is looking toward the future with Alton Robotics, developing AI-driven animatronics that redefine interactive entertainment. No matter where you visit us, Fazbear Entertainment remains committed to delivering safe, exciting, and unforgettable experiences. The best is yet to come!
