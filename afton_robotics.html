The Joy Of Creation

We here at Afton Robotics find joy in bringing these wonderful characters to life through the use of applied robotics. Since 1983, our innovative efforts to revolutionize the entertainment industry have not only led to breakthroughs in endoskeleton design for wide range application, but it has brought life to fantasy and fun. Our partners and partnering companies have showcased powerful hydraulics, modular springlock designs, and more through reconizable characters such as Spring Bonnie and Fredbear.

Meet The Team

With the guidance of our founder, William Afton, this team has built a connection between the entertainment industry through interactive preformance robotics. William Afton Designs the mechanisms behind each iterations of the endoskeletons, Henry Emily brings our creations the cheerful appearance kids have come to love, and Edwin Murray is responsible for assembling the animatronics and making their costumes. While partnering companies assist with materials, we've been proud partner companies with Fazbear Entertainment since 1987

Created independantly by our founder, William Afton, Circus Baby's Entertainment and Rental features our most ambitious endoskeleton designs yet! The mechanical parts are all connected by large cables that can replicate the muscles of a human being, while being three times as strong. With these features, it's meant to give our endoskeletons a wider range of articulation and therefore immersion. Most of our endoskeletons bridge gaps beyond our competitors, with the character 'ballora' being able to balance the entire structure on a single base, all while rotating repeatedly. Speed, computing, balance, endurance, and most of all, joy. We have surpassed the rest in all these fields.
